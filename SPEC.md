# ArtisanFlow Specification (SPEC.md)

## Mandatory Safety Parameters
* **Superfat Range:** Must strictly be between 5% and 8% (0.05 to 0.08).
* **Hardness Target:** Must be between 29 and 54.
* **Cleansing Target:** Must be between 12 and 22.
* **Conditioning Target:** Must be between 44 and 69.
* **Lye Type:** Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH) for solid bars.
* **Water-to-Lye Ratio:** 2:1 liquid concentration.

## Regulatory Constraints
* **Essential Oils:** Maximum safe usage threshold is 3% of total batch oil weight (IFRA Category 9 Compliance).
